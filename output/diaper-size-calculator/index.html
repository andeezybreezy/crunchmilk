Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know when to size up?

Signs you need the next size: frequent leaks or blowouts, red marks on thighs or waist, difficulty fastening tabs, the diaper looks too small. It's better to size up early than deal with leaks.

How many diapers does a baby use?

Newborn: 10-12/day. 3 months: 8-10/day. 6 months: 7-8/day. 12 months: 6-7/day. Toddler: 5-6/day. That's roughly 2,500-3,000 diapers in the first year, costing $600-900.

Are store brand diapers as good as name brand?

Many parents find store brands (Up&Up, Parent's Choice, Kirkland) work just as well at 40-50% less cost. Premium brands may fit better for some body types. Try a small pack before committing to a large box.
